About Me

I'm Donnie Reese, a full-stack web developer from Dallas, Texas. I've always been a tinkerer, passionate about learning and discovering new things and the ways in which they work. I've ended up learning new skills and playing with different ideas simply so I can teach others how to do the same.

I've always strived to find ways to make things useful, or atleast enjoyable, for others. That's why I'm a creator. You can keep up-to-date with some of my creations on Github. You can follow along with my journey at my website: donniereese.com or read some of what I write on Medium.

I'm making great things right now as a freelance developer and designer and, if you've got a team that's also making great things, you can email me at hello@donniereese.com or Contact Me directly.
